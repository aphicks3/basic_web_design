Welcome to My Website!

Hello! My name is Amanda. I live in the beautiful Sequatchie Valley in Tennessee. I have three siblings: one younger brother and two older sisters. I also have two pets: a cat named Louie, who is spoiled rotten and a beautiful dog named Luna. I like to read sometimes, and I also love going to the movies!

One thing about me is that I was diagnosed with type one diabetes when I was 13. However, I still live a pretty normal life. Here are some things to know.

* You can't get this type of diabetes from eating too much sugar.

* I have to give myself insulin with shots; I use Novolog and Levemir pens instead of vials.

* I wear a Dexcom G7 to monitor my glucose levels, so I don't have to prick my fingers every day.

* It's important to keep my blood sugar levels in a certain range to keep me healthy.

For more infromation: Mayo Clinic - Type One Diabetes
